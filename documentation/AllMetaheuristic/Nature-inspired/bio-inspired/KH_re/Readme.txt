The files in this zip archive are MATLAB m-files that can be used to study krill herd algorithm.

KH is the method that we invented and wrote about in the following paper:
Gandomi A.H., Alavi A.H., Krill Herd: A New Bio-Inspired Optimization Algorithm.
Communications in Nonlinear Science and Numerical Simulation, 
2012, 17 (12):4831-4845.
DOI: 10.1016/j.cnsns.2012.05.010
